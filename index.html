☆ Ouran High School Host Club ☆

CHARACTER

DESCRIPTION

Haruhi Fujioka ✎


♡ Haruhi is a highly intelligent 1st-year student from a middle-class family, though considered "poor" and "common" by the wealthy elite members attending Ouran Academy.

❝I'm not a hero, I'm an honors student...❞

Tamaki Suoh ✎


♡ Tamaki is the illegitimate son of Yuzuru Suoh (Ouran Academy's Chairman) and the president of the Ouran Host Club who is in Class 2-A, the same class as Kyoya Ootori -- the co-founder of the Host Club.

❝What does it mean to be strong? Is it something you can attain only by lying to yourself?❞

Kyoya Ootori ✎


♡ Kyoya is Tamaki's close friend and also the co-founder of the Host Club. As the third son of the wealthy and noble Ootori family, he believes he must surpass his two elder brothers if he wishes to please his father.

❝I have a daunting task: I must paint the perfect picture on a canvas that has already been placed inside a magnificent frame.❞

Takashi "Mori" Morinozuka ✎


♡ Mori is rarely seen without his cousin and best friend, Mitsukuni "Honey" Haninozuka, who is often found riding on his shoulders. He is a national champion in kendo and a skilled karate practitioner.

❝Every time you hurt others, you're the one who end up being hurt.❞

Mitsukuni "Honey" Haninozuka ✎


♡ Honey loves cute things and indulges in sweets of cake and candy on a near-constant basis; this, despite being smaller than average for his age and a martial arts champion. He is a perennial optimist with an innocent approach to life.

❝I like people who put in effort, even if they're not good at it.❞
